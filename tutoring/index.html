Tutoring

I offer tutoring services for mathematics and computer science. In high school, I passed 11 AP exams and received 5’s on 9 of them. I also scored in the 99th percentile of the ACT college admissions test. I have been tutoring students since middle school and have had the joy of seeing several of my students make tangible improvements in their academics, both through improved test scores and a deeper understanding of the material.

I can help students with SAT Math, ACT Math, Algebra 1, Algebra 2, Pre-Calculus, AP Calculus AB/BC, AP Statistics, and AP Computer Science.

My rate is $30/hour. Students receive 50% off on their first session. Please reach out to me at arthurwukn@email.virginia.edu if you are interested in a session.
